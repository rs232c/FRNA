10 football players up for Herald News Player of the Week Nov. 26-27

Herald News December 06, 2025 at 09:24 PM

FOOTBALL 10 football players up for Herald News Player of the Week Nov. 26-27 Steven Sanchez The Herald News Dec. 2, 2025, 2:20 p.m. ET Ten football players from the Fall River area have been placed on our Player of the Week ballot. This poll is for Thanksgiving Day performances from Nov. 26-27. Supporters of the schools — Atlantis Charter, Case, Diman, Durfee, Somerset Berkley and Westport — and student-athletes can visit heraldnews.com to vote for the person of their choosing. Somerset Berkley's Liam Meehan won the Nov. 13-14 poll with 70.8% of the vote. Somerset Berkley's field hockey netminder Mia Wiley won the Herald News Athlete poll for Nov. 10-15 with 78.69% of the vote.
